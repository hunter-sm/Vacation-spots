The Great Barrier Reef, The Great Barrier Reef is located in Cairns, Australia, it is a systym of complex coral reefs, shoals, and islets in the Pacific Ocean off the northeastern coast of Australia that is the longest and largest reef complex in the world. The Great Barrier Reef extends in a northwest and southeast direction for more than 1250 miles. At the barrier reef you can snorkele, go on water tours, go on nature and wild life tours, visit national parks, go hiking, and many more activities. The reef consists of some 2,100 individual reefs and some 800 boardering reefs.

The great barrier reef took hundreds of thousands of years to form, It is home to over 9,000 diffrent species of animals.
